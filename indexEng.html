Who am I?


Hi! My name is Miguel Teixeira.

A student whos at the moment finishing his journey on ESMAD - Superior School in Media Arts and Design and with this portfolio I'm sharing me and my work with the world!

I have proficiency with Adobe softwares (Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere e AfterEffects) for the development of graphic interfaces and editing in general.

On a 3D modeling level i'm proficient with blender and AutoDesk Maya for the creation of sceneries and characters.

The game development software that I use is Unity, however I look forward to learn in a near future how to work with Unreal Engine!

The programming languages that I have some knowledge are C#, P5JS (JavaScript Library), Processing and i'm on a journey to learn Python!
